How to NÜDL

1. How does payment work?

Hosts set a price that reimburses their grocery costs. Guests pay the hosts when they arrive. Cash and Venmo are the best options.

2. How can I tell who is attending the Mealshare?

Find the Mealshare listing on our website. You’ll be able to see who has RSVP’d.

3. I’m worried that I won’t know anyone there...

You should expect to meet students from different majors, different years and different backgrounds. Embracing the unique social atmosphere is important to the NÜDL experience.

4. Are drinks provided?

All NÜDL events are BYOB. Mealshare hosts are asked to provide water for all of their guests.

5. How long does a Mealshare last?

Typically lasts for about an hour, but sometimes they go longer. Feel free to leave at any point after the dishes have been cleared.

6. What if I feel uncomfortable during the Mealshare?

Awkward overload? If anything makes you seriously uncomfortable you can text or call us at (612) 308-6024 or (908) 591-7029.
